Consulting the Bones

Consulting the bones, or throwing the bones, is a sacred practice of divination passed down through generations. The bones are thrown and their positions interpreted to uncover spiritual truths and receive ancestral guidance. Each bone, shell, or object in the set represents different aspects of life, helping seekers find clarity, resolve inner turmoil, and make important decisions.
